Tap Miner-Lumberjack: Mining Clicker

Cut trees, beat stones and grow a beard in a new clicker game.

Tap Miner-Lumberjack: Mining Clicker is a simple clicker game. You are on an island with two locations with trees and stones and a merchant on the pier. Cut trees and hammer stones. Buy new tools, clothes, and potions. Improve the strength and dexterity of the character and grow a beard.
