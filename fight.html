Last Fight: Action RPG

Can you defeat all enemies on your way and reach the end?

Last Fight - Action RPG game with a unique real-time combat system.   You are thrown onto the shore of an unfriendly island. Try to reach the end fighting with different opponents on your way.   The game has a unique battle system with three directions of strikes and blocks. Reflect strikes in the right direction otherwise skip the beat. Reaching a new level, improve strength, agility, vitality and endurance.   Move around the island discovering new locations with new opponents. Also, along with some locations will open merchants. Get for victories or buy new armor and weapons. Restore health with different foods. Good luck!
